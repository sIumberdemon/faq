FAQ #2 || How to become staff
In order to become staff, you must be in the server longer than a week and be well known by the staff and be active and follow the rules. and then you may make a modmail and say you'd like to apply for event staff or moderator.

And you will be updated on if you are able to apply for said position.

FAQ #3 || Moderation Actions
When you reach 3 warnings you get muted automatically for 15 minutes, and if you get punished too much in a short period of time a moderator will kick you and if you continue breaking rules after the kick you will be temp banned for a week.
